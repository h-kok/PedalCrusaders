About Us


We are about all things that empower mountain biking, and hope that maybe just one of our vids encourages someone somewhere to jump on a bike and ride. The love goes both ways. Throwing ourselves into mountain biking has made us stronger and we have been to many great locations for our adrenaline hit.

We're about travelling/adventuring around and finding car camping spots. We're hoping to share our adventures with everyone and share the good times of mountain biking, as well as some of our knowledge in different areas. Amanda's a Physio and Dave never shuts up.

By all means if you have a trail that we should know about, please get in contact and we will do our best to come ride with you.

Keep crusading and pedals out!

-Amanda & Dave-
